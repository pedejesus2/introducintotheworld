Why cats are wonderful

Every cat is a mood board—playful, contemplative, mischievous, regal. Their individuality makes the friendship feel personal and earned.

FAQ

Do cats really always land on their feet?

They have a "righting reflex" that helps, but heights and surfaces matter. Keep windows secured and provide safe climbing options.

Are cats aloof?

Many are affectionate once they trust you. Cats communicate subtly—slow blinks, tail language, head bumps.

Indoor or outdoor?

Indoor lives are typically safer and longer. Consider leashed adventures or a secure catio for enrichment.
